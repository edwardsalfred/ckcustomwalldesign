CK Custom Accent & Feature Wall Design (CK Wall Design) is an exquisite interior wall design company based in Houston, Texas. Our goal is to bring beauty and style to the room by creating beautiful works of art on the walls that accentuate any design style.

The company was built out of passion and love for art and interior design. Clinton is the artist. His patience, dedication, and meticulous disposition is the reason our designs come out flawlessly executed, and the reason clients send us pictures months later when their walls still look the day we left. Kerry is the designer. Her love for interior design and her quest to turn an ordinary home into a model, personalised home is how this business began.

We are both registered nurses. The same care and client-focused service we brought to patients, we bring to your home. It is, in its own way, the same job: you arrive, you listen, you work with great attention, and you leave the person in a better state than you found them.

We work on every style: glam, farmhouse, mid-century, eclectic, classic, industrial. But we only work one way: carefully, from consultation to final walkthrough.
