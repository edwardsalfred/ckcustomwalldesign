01 · What We Do

Every technique. One studio.

From century-old wainscoting profiles to modern geometric feature walls. Each finish is measured, mitred, and painted in your home.

01

Wainscoting & Board & Batten

Classic, panel-moulded, and modern profiles. Measured and mitred on-site for a finish that reads as part of the architecture.

02

Geometric & Modern Feature Walls

Chevron, herringbone, and custom geometric panels, computed to scale with the room's proportion, lighting, and symmetry.

03

Shiplap & Wall Panels

Horizontal shiplap, vertical slat, and custom panel runs. Tight seams, perfect reveals, paint-grade or stain-grade.

04

Ceiling & Kitchen Island Designs

Coffered, boxed, and beamed ceilings. Panelled island faces and waterfall details that turn the kitchen into a feature room.

05

Fine Paints of Europe

Certified application of the world's most storied paint line. Depth, sheen, and colour unmatched by anything off a big-box shelf.

06

Glitter & Stencil Specialty

Signature glitter accent walls and stencilled designs for nurseries, dressing rooms, and statement headboards.

07

General & Custom Painting

Interior repaints done with trim-grade prep and a finish coat we'd put our name on. Because we do.

08

Workshops & Consultations

In-person and virtual consultations. Training courses for designers and homeowners who want to learn the craft from us.

02 · The CK Method

A way of working, not a job to be done.

Three phases. Every client, every project, every time, so the result is never an accident.

01 Consult

Listen, measure, sketch.

A 30-minute in-home or virtual consultation. We walk the room, understand your intention, and leave with measurements and a firm quote. No mystery pricing.

02 Design

Proportion, profile, palette.

We produce a to-scale schematic, select the profile and materials, and lock the Fine Paints of Europe colour with you before a single board is cut.

03 Install

On-site, careful, complete.

Most projects finish in one to five days. We tape, dust-sheet, and walk every room with you before we pack up. It isn't done until you say it's done.
