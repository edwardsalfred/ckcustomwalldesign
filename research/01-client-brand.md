## What's Strong Already

1. Real founders with a story — Clinton & Kerry have authentic warmth
2. Transparent pricing — most competitors hide this
3. Wide service range with specialty finishes (Fine Paints of Europe is a genuine luxury signal)
4. Social proof via active Instagram/TikTok
5. A second revenue stream (courses) most competitors don't have

## What's Weak / Hurting Conversion

1. **Wix template feel** — plateaus the brand at "local small business" when pricing + pedigree suggest premium
2. **No hero statement** — visitors land without a clear value prop or "why CK"
3. **Portfolio is an uncategorized grid** — no rooms, no styles, no story
4. **CTAs compete** — consultation vs. courses with equal weight
5. **No process/education** — clients buying $5k+ custom work need to see the method
6. **No founder imagery/video** — story is in text only; Clinton & Kerry should be the face of the brand
7. **Zero motion/scroll craft** — no sense of editorial elevation
